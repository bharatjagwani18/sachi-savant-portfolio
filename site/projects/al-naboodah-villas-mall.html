Concept design and detailing for a villa typology and community mall at Al Khawaneej Avenue — an upcoming residential community by Al Naboodah Group.

The villas pair warm neutrals with landscape views framed by full-height glazing, while the mall composes daylight, timber ceilings and marble surfaces into an open community destination.

  • Prepared and delivered a comprehensive detailed drawing package for the complete villa typology
  • Assisted with design development in SketchUp
  • Coordinated with the visualisation team for final renders
